Non-Food Inflation and Alcoholic Beverages (Year-on-Year)

As can be seen from the above figure, both the non-food year-on-year inflation rate and Food inflation
rate increased in April-ETY2017 as compared to last month. The Non-Food inflation increased by 17.9
percent during the same period. Particularly, Alcoholic Beverages and Tobacco increased by (16.5%);
Housing, water, electricity & other fuel component (18.3%); Furnishings, household equipment & rou-
tine maintenance of the House (16.5%); Health (10.5%); Transport (40.5%); Communication (7.4%);
Education (23.2%); Restaurants and hotels (18.3%) and Miscellaneous goods and services (18.0%) have
contributed for the rise in non-food inflation rate in April EFY2017.

Month-to-Month Inflation Rate

The month-to-month general inflation rate for April EFY2017 has increased by 0.1 percent compared to what was
observed last month. During the same month, the food and non-alcoholic component of the inflation increased

by 0.1 percent whereas the non-food component for its part increased by 0.2 percent.
